The band

Radiohead is a British alternative rock band formed in 1985, known for their musical innovation and introspective lyrics. They are celebrated for their constant evolution and influence on contemporary music.

Discography

  • Pablo Honey (1993)
  • The Bends (1995)
  • Ok Computer (1997)
  • Kid A (2000)
  • Amnesiac (2001)
  • Hail To The Thief (2003)
  • In Rainbows (2007)
  • The King Of Limbs (2011)
  • A Moon Shaped Pool (2016)

Members

  • Thom Yorke (singer)
  • Phil Selway (drums)
  • Johnny Greenwood (lead guitar)
  • Colin Greenwood (bass)
  • Ed O'Brien (guitar)

In recent years, Radiohead has been inactive as a band, but its members have focused on individual projects: In 2024, rumors emerged of a Radiohead reunion after the band reportedly rehearsed together, sparking expectations for new material in 2025.
